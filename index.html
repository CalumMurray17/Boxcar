Here's the Pattern — and Why Boxcar Breaks It.

Same market, different failure modes. The demand was real — the execution wasn't.

Leap Transit
Feb 2015 – Mar 2015 · R.I.P.

Luxury SF buses, $6/ride, Wi-Fi + coffee. Routes 1 and 2. Same Finance/Tech customer.

BR
Bridj
2014 – May 2017 · R.I.P.

⚰ Demand too slow, buses unfilled. GM pulled out. B2C couldn't cover fixed costs. Shut down May 2017.
